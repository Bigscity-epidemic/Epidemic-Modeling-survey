Project Description

Since its discovery in December 2019, Corona Virus Disease 2019, COVID-19 caused by SARS-CoV-2, referred to as "new coronary pneumonia", There is already a global pandemic.

Before the new coronavirus vaccine and specific drugs are successfully developed and widely used, public policy intervention is the primary means of preventing and controlling the development and spread of the epidemic. To study the mathematical modeling of the new crown pneumonia epidemic, that is, to describe the relationship between natural, environmental, social, economic, medical, prevention and control factors and the spread of the epidemic through mathematical models, analyze and predict the development trend of the epidemic, and carry out the possible effects of prevention and control policies Simulation evaluation is an important basis for formulating public policies for scientific epidemic prevention and control.

As a new infectious disease, people’s understanding of it is still very insufficient, and the various socio-economic impacts brought by the new coronary pneumonia have not been encountered before. People are concerned about the new crown epidemic itself and its occurrence. The limitations of the understanding of the impact have brought many challenges to the modeling of the new crown pneumonia epidemic.

The research results of this project include not only the prediction and simulation analysis models and software tools of the new crown pneumonia epidemic, but also the theoretical knowledge that promotes people’s understanding of the new crown pneumonia epidemic and similar emerging infectious diseases. Carry out specific practical applications in the control work of China, Russia and Brazil.
